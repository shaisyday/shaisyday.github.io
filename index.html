Welcome

We are Evolutionary Gaming - a gaming community focused on helping new players learn basic game mechanics. We focus on DayZ and offer various servers that feature balanced traders, no base raiding, and absolutely no PVP. Evolutionary also boasts several long term players who have made our server their permanent home. Our helpful community is always available to chat in game or on Discord.

We aim to provide a safe, non-toxic space for new and experienced players alike. We encourage the exploration of different maps, a learning environment, and an engaged community.

Rules

Evolutionary Gaming is designed to be a safe server with rules to support that experience. The rules are designed to reduce lag, keep the servers running smoothly, and be fair and welcoming. Server rules are in place to promote common courtesy and to help us achieve the goals Evolutionary Gaming has set for all servers. All rules are created for the betterment of the server and to support the success of the players. All rules are subject to change with or without notice. Report any and all violations as quickly as you can via the ticket system on Discord. Evolutionary Gaming servers are strictly PVE only. You must be 17 or over to play on any Evolutionary Gaming server. Be a good person. Admin decisions are final and at our discretion. The server panels on each server are considered to be the most accurate and up to date. We will defer to them at all times. You can access the server panels in game by clicking 'esc' or 'pause' on your keyboard.

Meet the Team

Founded in 2021, Evolutionary Gaming boasts a stellar team of admins and mods with varied professional and gaming backgrounds. Texas Texas is the server & Discord owner and hails from Texas. She started the Evolutionary Gaming servers to build an awesome, friendly, helpful community in which to play DayZ in a non-toxic and non-lethal way. Admins The team includes a host of amazing people who make the servers possible by testing, modding, coding, creating, and maintaining our maps.
