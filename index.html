Welcome to Evolutionary Gaming - a gaming community focused on helping new players learn basic game mechanics. We focus on DayZ and offer various servers that feature balanced traders, no base raiding, and absolutely no PVP. Evolutionary also boasts several long term players who have made our server their permanent home. Our helpful community is always available to chat in game or on Discord.

We aim to provide a safe, non-toxic space for new and experienced players alike. We encourage the exploration of different maps, a learning environment, and an engaged community.

Community Rules

Evolutionary Gaming is designed to be a safe server with rules to support that experience. The rules are designed to reduce lag, keep the servers running smoothly, and be fair and welcoming.

Server rules are in place to promote common courtesy and to help us achieve the goals Evolutionary Gaming has set for all servers. All rules are created for the betterment of the server and to support the success of the players. All rules are subject to change with or without notice.

  • PVE only
  • 17+
  • Be respectful
  • Report issues through Discord
  • Admin decisions are final

The server panels on each server are considered to be the most accurate and up to date. We will defer to them at all times. You can access the server panels in game by clicking 'esc' or 'pause' on your keyboard.
