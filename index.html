Welcome

We are Evolutionary Gaming - a gaming community focused on helping new players learn basic game mechanics. We focus on DayZ and offer various servers that feature balanced traders, no base raiding, and absolutely no PVP. Evolutionary also boasts several long term players who have made our server their permanent home. Our helpful community is always available to chat in game or on Discord.

We aim to provide a safe, non-toxic space for new and experienced players alike. We encourage the exploration of different maps, a learning environment, and an engaged community.
